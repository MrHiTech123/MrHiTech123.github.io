shoot(thing_youre_shooting, damage, speed, angle=0)

thing_youre_shooting = the projectile. The only currently available option is "fire".

damage = how much damage the thing you shoot should deal

speed = the speed of the projectile in pixels per frame (1 pixel per frame = 30 pixels per second)

angle (optional) = how many radians clockwise of your mouse the fireball should shoot; the current default is 0, which means that the projectile will be shot in the direction of your mouse.

For each projectile a spell creates, the spell costs 1 mana per damage it deals and 2 mana per pixel it travels per frame. Angle does not contribute to mana cost. The player has a maximum mana of 100 and recovers 1 mana per frame. Spells also require 1 frame to cast per mana they cost. For example, a spell that costs 60 mana takes 60 frames (2 seconds) to cast.
